Somerville Community Choice Electricity (CCE) Aggregation

The Somerville Community Choice Electricity (CCE) Aggregation began July 2017. See the Somerville CCE page for more information.

This program aims to achieve stable electricity pricing, increase Somerville’s commitment to local renewable energy, and provide residents an option to opt up to 100% local renewable energy as their source of electricity. This calculator provides an easy way to compare how the options will affect your bill, with a goal of showing that the benefits of 100% local renewable energy can be achieved with a relatively modest increase in a household’s bill.

Calculating an electrical bill

A monthly bill is calculated as follows:

kWh * Generation Rate + kWh * Delivery Rate + Customer Charge

The Generation Rate is what is being changed by the CCE plan. By opting up to 100%, only the Generation Rate aspect would increase.

Delivery Rates are composed of multiple items, see your Eversource bill for details. The delivery rates shown in the calculator by default are from January 2018 for a residential customer.
